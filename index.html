4/30/2018-5/4/2018

Lychee Fundraiser and Arduinos




As you may know, we hosted the lychee fundraiser not only to raise funds for the club but also to help people who wanted to buy Arduinos fund their purchase. The little infographic I made above explained how the process worked and how it helped us save money.

Anyway, tax included, the Arduino Uno Rev3 came to about $24. Our awesome treasurer, Stella Saw, collected money for the lychee, but not the Arduino, so if you planned to buy one, you messaged Stella on Facebook to let her know that you were interested in being part of the group order, and she told you how much money to bring to the meeting on Thursday, April 19.

4/18/17

Four officers, Patrick Liu, Sandra Tang, Stella Saw and Samuel Berkun, volunteered at Field Elementary School's Science Night in Pasadena.
